Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense entries you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and let you filter and review your spending history. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No expense data or personal account information is shared with any third party. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or external tracking tools.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
